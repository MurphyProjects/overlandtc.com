About

Murphy Projects is the operating entity behind everything Colin Murphy builds. Based in Ontario, Canada, it exists to create businesses that are coherent, intentional, and built to last.

Each venture draws from real experience — the classroom, the trail, the lab with a tennis ball in its mouth. The goal isn't to scale fast. It's to build well.

Incorporated as 17606656 Canada Inc., Murphy Projects provides the legal and operational structure for ventures in education, publishing, retail, and digital products.
